Detailed description

In the Moscow Metro, since August 10, 2010, regular operation of the Yezh and Yezh1 cars has been suspended on all lines, except for the Arbatsko-Pokrovskaya line, from which trains left only on August 23, 2010. They were replaced by "Numbered" on the Sokolnicheskaya line (September 2008), on "Rusichi" on the Filyovskaya line (from September 12, 2009) and the Arbatsko-Pokrovskaya line. The last car of this type in Moscow was car No. 5170, which was preserved for the museum. The cars "Ezh" and "Ezh1" were also operated in the Tbilisi metro. All operating cars of the "Ezh" type currently operate only as intermediate cars, the last time the cars of the "Ezh" type were used as head cars in 2015 in Kiev.
