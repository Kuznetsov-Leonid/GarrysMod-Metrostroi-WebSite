The first ideas for the project appeared at Black Phoenix around 2005-2006, around this time the general idea was created. Around this time, he was working on the rp_subtransit map for Garry's mod, which at that time was one of the first maps for Garry's mod, which contained working trains moving according to the physics of the game Half life 2. The Garry's mod platform was selected for ready-made support for online play , a user-friendly Lua interface that enabled live code editing in multiplayer, which made Garry's Mod the perfect platform for prototyping Metrostroi. After working on the idea in 2005-2006, there were still different prototypes of such physics of train movement, including separately from Garry's Mod.
