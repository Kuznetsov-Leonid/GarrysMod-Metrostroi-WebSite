Short description

The E-type cars had a different distance between the side sliding doors compared to the D-type cars and therefore were not suitable for closed-type stations of the Moscow metro. In addition, the E type cars were not equipped with an automatic steering system for more accurate stopping at such stations. At the moment, they are the oldest rolling stock units currently operated with passengers in the CIS. In this regard, the Mytishchi machine-building plant redesigned type E cars, creating three new types of cars: head cars of the Ema type (factory designation 81-705) - equipped with automatic guidance devices PMSAUP systems and devices for receiving signals from track sensors;

Detailed description

The carriages of the New Year's train were decorated with images of red-breasted bullfinches, spruce branches, refined snowflakes and an inscription with the numbers of the coming year on a white and gray background in the form of snow, and the salon was also decorated with tinsel and electric garlands. In this design, the train ran only in December and January, and the rest of the time it was transferred to one of the depots for storage. It originally ran on the Circle Line in a reduced six-car train from December 28, 2016 to January 15, 2017, and it was the only train of this model on the line. Ema cars are the oldest cars in the CIS countries that carry passengers Ema cars are not compatible with E cars, therefore it is not possible to couple them There were tail cars Emx, which, unlike the head cars, did not have an automatic steering system, but only devices for controlling it. However, they were later abandoned and re-equipped as head These cars have an automatic informant Tanks with KVL have a safety pedal instead of a vigilance pedal, which must always be kept pressed, otherwise the train will not start moving. The vigilance pedal is replaced by a limited travel button. KVL and NVL are abbreviations of the lines of the St. Petersburg metro. KVL - Kirovsko-Vyborgskaya (red) line, NVL - Nevsko-Vasileostrovskaya (green) These cars are equipped with an automatic steering system. It was planned to operate them on the NVL, since almost all the stations on this line are closed-type stations and need very precise braking.
