New game features

  • Train spawner is much lighter and cleaner. Now you just need to choose the train you are interested in with the number of wagons and press spawn!
  • Breaking the seals is now done with the right mouse button!
  • Tooltips have changed
  • The reversible grip is now numbered for each player
  • New function of choice of starting the train when spawning - from completely disabled in the "brick" state, to ready to move on the train line.
  • Each type of train now has its own unique characteristics and driving style.
  • The train hitch now has a convenient action menu. We've also added a camera aimed at it to make it easier for you to link trains!
  • Global sound update - we have created a new sound system for the train in motion, unique for each type of train, added new different types of sounds for engines, traffic noise, replacement valves, compressors, * Sounds of contactors, HF, buttons and switches, new sounds of BPSN, and much more.
  • The distance between the cars has been changed. Now it is real, and not fitted to the horizontal lift of the gm_metrostroi map
  • Simulation of voltage sag in control circuits and interior lighting lamps.
  • Correct operation of valves 334 and 013, time delays, relay activation, sounds of relays and contactors
  • Each car has a unique shade, cars with the same color will have slight color differences due to different types of paint and the degree of wear.
  • Wall diagrams, plates of route numbers and terminal stations. Seems like it's for passengers?
